About Greif

What's in a name? Greif rhymes with life and is pronounced "gryfe" — a global leader in industrial packaging built on innovation and service.

Vision & Purpose

Our Vision

To be the advantage that helps our customers win.

Our Purpose

Creating packaging solutions for life's essentials.

Our People

Over 14,000 colleagues across more than 250 facilities in 37 countries.

Company Overview

Greif is a leading supplier of industrial packaging products and services, growing from Cleveland, Ohio to a global footprint across more than thirty countries.

We are innovators — building packaging that's sustainable, innovative, and focused on circularity.

The Greif Way — Our Values

People Focused

We lead through our culture, focusing on people every step of the way.

Zero Harm

We think safety in every situation.

Servant Leadership

We prioritize the needs and growth of our colleagues and customers.

Customer Driven

We prioritize the needs of our customers.

Action Biased

We are proactive, not reactive.

Personal Accountability

Greif is known around the world for integrity.

Our History

1877 — Vanderwyst and Greif cooperage shop in Cleveland, Ohio.

2001 — Acquisition of Van Leer Industrial; Greif, Inc.

2024 — Greif acquires Ipackchem.
