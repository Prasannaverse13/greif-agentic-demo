About Greif

What's in a name? Greif rhymes with life and is pronounced "gryfe" — a global leader in industrial packaging built on innovation and service.

Vision & Purpose

Our Vision

To be the advantage that helps our customers win.

Our Purpose

Creating packaging solutions for life's essentials.

Our People

Over 14,000 colleagues across more than 250 facilities in 37 countries — protecting, engaging, and developing an inclusive culture.

Company Overview

Greif is a leading supplier of industrial packaging products and services, growing from its beginnings in Cleveland, Ohio, to a global footprint across more than thirty countries and 200-plus locations.

We are innovators — building packaging that's sustainable, innovative, and focused on circularity. Greif produces steel, plastic and fibre drums, intermediate bulk containers, reconditioned containers, flexible products, containerboard, tubes and cores, and a diverse mix of specialty products.

Our History

1877 — William Greif and Albert Vanderwyst open "Vanderwyst and Greif" cooperage shop in Cleveland, Ohio.
1923 — First bitumen steel drum produced, still part of the portfolio today.
1998 — The Greif Way is first published with a clear vision for customer service excellence.
2001 — Acquisition of Van Leer Industrial doubles company size; name changes to Greif, Inc.
2019 — Greif acquires Caraustar Industries in the largest deal in company history.
2024 — Greif acquires Ipackchem, a global leader in jerrycan and small plastic container manufacturing.

Giving Back

Greif is committed to building a better tomorrow by giving back to our communities today — supporting education, health, and social services.
